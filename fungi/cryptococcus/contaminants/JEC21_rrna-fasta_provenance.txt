This file was created by Edward Wallace in 2018, ribosomal RNA transcripts selected from the genome assembly and annotation of Cryptococcus deneoformans strain JEC21.
The genome of Cryptococcus deneoformans JEC21 (serotype D) was sequenced by TIGR. The data (genome assembly and gene predictions) were obtained from GenBank (record PRJNA10698).
