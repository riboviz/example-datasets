This file was created by Edward Wallace in 2018, ribosomal RNA transcripts selected from the CNA3 genome assembly and annotation of Cryptococcus neoformans strain H99.
The full annotation is available in Genbank under project accession PRJNA411. The genome is also available at FungiDb.

Citation:

Janbon G et al., "Analysis of the genome and transcriptome of Cryptococcus neoformans var. grubii reveals complex RNA expression and microevolution leading to virulence attenuation.", PLoS Genet, 2014 Apr 17;10(4):e1004261
