This file was created by Edward Wallace in 2018, ribosomal RNA and tRNA transcripts selected from the genome assembly and annotation of Neurospora crassa strain NC12.

The data were downloaded from Ensembl fungi, http://fungi.ensembl.org/Neurospora_crassa/Info/Index

The genome sequence of the filamentous fungus Neurospora crassa.
Galagan JE, Calvo SE, Borkovich KA, Selker EU, Read ND, Jaffe D, FitzHugh W, Ma LJ, Smirnov S, Purcell S et al. 2003. Nature. 422:859-868.
